About Red Spider Lily

Lycoris radiata, known as the red spider lily, red magic lily, corpse flower, or equinox flower, is a plant in the amaryllis family, Amaryllidaceae.

It originates from China, Japan, Korea, and Nepal and has since spread to the United States and beyond. It blooms in late summer or autumn, often after heavy rainfall.
